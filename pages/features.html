Features

CollieQ makes it easy to manage queues with the touch of a button!

As simple as pressing a button
All you have to do is press a button when a customer enters and exits, and we’ll take care of the rest.
Gain insights on your customers’ needs
Easily view store analytics such as average queue times and most popular days, so you can make business decisions based on accurate data to maximize your profits.
The comfort of a virtual queue
Put your customers at ease, letting them queue up from the safety and comfort of their car or a nearby cafe.
Peace of mind
Provide your customers with their exact positions in line and accurate wait times.
