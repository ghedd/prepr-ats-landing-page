Features

Accessible Store Information

Customers can get information such as store hours, contact information and other store locations all at the touch of a button.

Accurate Queue Time

Provide customers with accurate queue time estimates and exact positions in line and ability to cancel for complete transparency from both parties.

Easy Setup

As a queue manager, simply enter your store information and update the maximum capacity of customers allowed in the store and you are good to go!

Simple Queue Management

Simply press on the minus or plus sign when customers enter or leave the shop while having queue time estimates available to you at the palm of your hand!

Analytics Available

Get data from the store's past day, week, month or year about information such as daily customers, average wait times and more- available from our premium enterprise members.
