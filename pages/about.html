Our Misson

In today's pandemic-stricken society, businesses have had to face challenges in terms of managing foot traffic throughout their stores and restaurants. Our aim is to implement an easy to use system that helps manage crowds and allow businesses an easier time complying with regulations and prevent the spread of disease. We would like to assuage public concerns regarding their health and safety while minimizing the impact of the pandemic on brick-and-mortar consumerism. We want to help businesses reduce stress by improving efficiency and reducing crowding from essential stores.
